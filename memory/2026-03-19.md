  - `agents.defaults.memorySearch.fallback = "none"`
  - `agents.defaults.memorySearch.remote.baseUrl = "https://openrouter.ai/api/v1"`
  - `agents.defaults.memorySearch.remote.apiKey = <OpenRouter key>`
